Most validation frameworks ask:

  • Is there a market?
  • Will people pay?
  • Can you build it?

They don't ask:

  • Will the required GTM make you miserable?
  • How long until you discover the mismatch?
  • Are you building for the wrong founder (you)?
Result: You build something that works — but requires distribution behaviors you won't sustain. You usually discover this 60—90 days in, after committing time and energy.

Standard validation does not measure founder—GTM fit.

Standard validation tells you what to build.

It doesn't tell you if you'll hate how you have to sell it.

The missing question:
"Will I hate the GTM this requires?"

That's what this diagnostic answers.

What This Is

  • A Founder—GTM Misery Risk Diagnostic.

What This Is NOT

  • It only classifies misalignment risk between what the GTM structurally requires and how you actually work.
