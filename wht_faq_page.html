Methodology

The system uses deterministic rules to:

  • Map your idea to one of five primary GTM patterns
  • Identify the structural behaviors each pattern requires
  • Compare those requirements against your stated working preferences and historical tolerance patterns
  • Flag conflicts where the GTM demands behaviors you explicitly avoid or cannot sustain

There is no AI interpretation. Same inputs → same verdict.
