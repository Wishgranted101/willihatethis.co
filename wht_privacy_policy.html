Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 11, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Will I Hate This? ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our diagnostic service.

In short: We collect only what's necessary to deliver your diagnostic. We don't sell your data. We don't spam you. We don't share your individual responses.

Information We Collect

Assessment Responses

When you complete the diagnostic, we collect your answers to the 18 assessment questions. These responses include information about your idea's structure, your GTM approach, and your working patterns.

Contact Information

We collect your email address to deliver your diagnostic report and send you a copy of your results.

Payment Information

Payment processing is handled by our third-party payment processor. We do not store your full credit card details. We receive only transaction confirmation and basic billing information.

Technical Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Deliver your diagnostic report via email and on-screen display
  • Process your payment through our payment processor
  • Improve the diagnostic methodology using aggregated, anonymized data
  • Send critical service updates (e.g., changes to the diagnostic, policy updates)
  • Respond to support requests when you contact us
  • Prevent fraud and abuse of the service

What We Do NOT Do

We do not sell your data.

We do not share your individual assessment responses publicly.

We do not send marketing emails without your explicit consent.

We do not track you across other websites.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

Service Providers

We share information with third-party service providers who help us operate our service, including:

  • Payment processors (for transaction processing)
  • Email delivery services (for sending your diagnostic report)
  • Hosting providers (for website and data storage)

These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information and use it only for the services they provide to us.

Aggregated Data

We may use aggregated, anonymized data to improve the diagnostic methodology. For example: "Founders with X profile typically experience Y outcome with Z GTM pattern." This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or legal process, or if necessary to protect our rights, property, or safety.

Data Retention

We retain your assessment data and diagnostic report indefinitely to allow you to reference your results. You may request deletion of your data at any time by emailing support@willihatethis.com.

Technical logs and analytics data are retained for up to 90 days.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data — Request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct your data — Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Delete your data — Request deletion of your assessment responses and diagnostic report
  • Opt out of communications — Unsubscribe from non-essential emails

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@willihatethis.com.

Cookies and Tracking

We use essential cookies to operate the service (e.g., maintaining your session, remembering your theme preference). We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

We use basic analytics to understand how people use our site (page views, time spent, referral sources). This helps us improve the service.

Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data, including encryption for data in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If we discover we have collected information from a child, we will delete it immediately.

International Users

If you are accessing our service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. By using our service, you consent to this transfer.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. If we make material changes, we will notify you via email.

Continued use of the service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, contact us at:

Email: support@willihatethis.com

Response time: Within 48 hours
